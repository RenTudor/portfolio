Gorgeous holiday cottage by the beach

Our gorgeous seaside holiday rental cottage is set right above Aberporth's family friendly Dolwen beach - recently voted one of the best in Wales. Sleeping up to 6 in three bedrooms (two doubles and a twin), plus two more in a comfy sofa bed in it's own room. Enjoy stunning sea views from almost every room. Spot dolphins in the bay from the sunny conservatory or curl up with a book on the upstairs window seats.

Feeling energetic? You can have your toes in the sand in seconds and swim, sunbathe or explore the rock pools. Want a walk? The Wales Coast Path passes the house and leads to Tresaith to the North (the first mile of this path is inclusive access) and in the other direction a spectacular clifftop walk to pretty Mwnt beach with it's tiny whitewashed church.

The large enclosed garden makes it ideal for families with space to play. Furnished to the highest standards and with a fully equipped kitchen it's an ideal base to explore the West Coast of Wales. The village amenities are within easy walking distance with a small cafe and beach shop a few doors away, a pub and chip shop around the corner and local shop, post office and chemist in the village centre a ten minute walk around the back of the bigger Dyffryn beach.

Aberporth achieved "Plastic Free" status from Surfers Against Sewage in January 2018 for their efforts to reduce plastic pollution particularly from single use plastics. Many of the businesses in the village have 'plastic free' accreditation and Golwg y Graig seaside holiday cottage was awarded 'plastic free' status in March 2019 shortly after opening, showing that reducing plastic can go hand in hand with a luxurious seaside holiday.

Prices from £550. Sorry no pets at this time.
