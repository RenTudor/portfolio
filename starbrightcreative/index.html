About Me



My name is Seren Tudor, and I am a web developer specialising in Front End development.


I come from 5 years in retail, but my passion always lay in the more creative side of things. Making visually appealing displays, engaging copy for social media, and heck, customer interactions are an art-form in themselves. Then I became slightly obsessed with fictional tech giant Tony Stark(Iron Man), and had myself an epiphany. I could learn to code!


Starting with CodeAcademy's free courses, I got hooked on the front-end languages. It mixed my creative flair with my secret desire to be Iron Man, and I wanted to do more. Now, I am studying with JustIt on their Developers Programme, improving my skills and turning my passions into a reality.


I currently live in Bracknell, Berkshire with a tonne of books and my house rabbit, Tommy.
